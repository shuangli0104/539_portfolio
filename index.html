About Iceland

Iceland is often called the Land of Ice and Fire, a land of pristine and unspoiled nature.

Iceland is a country of extreme geological contrasts. Widely known as the "Land of Fire and Ice", Iceland is home to some of the largest glaciera in Europe, and the world's most active volcano.
